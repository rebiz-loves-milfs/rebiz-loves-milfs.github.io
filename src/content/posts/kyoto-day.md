## Morning

The shinkansen from Tokyo takes about two and a half hours. I had a bento. I watched the fields go by.

## Afternoon

Fushimi Inari is always crowded at the entrance, but climb high enough and you get stretches of path where you can hear just the wind and the distant bells.

## Tea

I found a small place near Nishiki Market. The matcha was proper — not the tourist kind. I sat there for an hour.

## Evening

The train home. Watching the lights blur. Already thinking about the next visit.
