# Dear Future Me

If you're reading this, the date is at least June 1st, 2026.

I'm writing this in May, not sure what the next few weeks will bring. There's something satisfying about leaving a message for yourself — like a time capsule sealed with intent.

## What I'm hoping for

By the time you read this, I hope:

- The blog is still going strong
- You've finished at least two books on the reading list
- You've shipped something you're genuinely proud of
- The okchura ramen place opened the second location

## What I was working on when I sealed this

Right now the design system is coming together nicely. The atmosphere loop is running — sometimes rain, sometimes fireflies, sometimes just the sakura petals in the silence.

The AniList stats widget came out well. Spice and Wolf's genre distribution looks exactly how I expected.

## A reminder

Keep writing. Not for anyone else — for yourself. The act of putting thoughts into words clarifies them. Even when nobody reads it, it's worth it.

See you on the other side of May. 🌸

— Past Rebiz
